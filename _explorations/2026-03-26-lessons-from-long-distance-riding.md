Long rides are a systems problem in disguise.

## What transfers to engineering

- Preparation beats improvisation under pressure.
- Energy management matters more than brute effort.
- Good decisions come from clear constraints, not optimism.

Riding also improves patience: you cannot force road conditions, but you can adapt to them continuously.
