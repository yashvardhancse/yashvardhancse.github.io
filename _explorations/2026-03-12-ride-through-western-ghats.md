I started before sunrise and crossed long stretches of mist, forest bends, and empty roads.

The ride reminded me that pace matters more than speed. Consistent rhythm, alertness, and small checkpoints make long routes manageable.

## Reflection

Good rides are not about chasing distance records. They are about attention: road texture, weather shifts, and fatigue signals.
