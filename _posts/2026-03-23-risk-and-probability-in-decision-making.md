Decision quality and outcome quality are not always the same.

## A small framework

- Define possible outcomes
- Assign rough probabilities
- Estimate expected value
- Plan downside control before upside pursuit

## Common mistakes I try to avoid

- Confusing confidence with probability
- Ignoring base rates
- Making high-variance choices without position sizing

This note is about decision hygiene, not trading advice.
